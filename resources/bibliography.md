# Bibliography — Iterative Functional Equations

## Primary References

1. **Vladimir V. Kruchinin, Dmitry V. Kruchinin** (2013). *Composita and its Properties*. arXiv:1103.2582v2.
   - Introduces the composita \(F^{\Delta}(n,k)\) of ordinary generating functions.

2. **Dmitry V. Kruchinin, Vladimir V. Kruchinin** (2013). *Method for solving an iterative functional equation \(A^{2^n}(x) = F(x)\)*. arXiv:1302.1986v2.
   - Extends the composita method to iterative functional equations.

## External Resources

3. **Lars Regløs**. *f(f(x))* — https://reglos.de/lars/ffx.html
   - Interactive exploration of functional square roots.

7. **P. Flajolet, R. Sedgewick** (2008). *Analytic Combinatorics*. Cambridge University Press.
8. **H. S. Wilf** (1994). *Generatingfunctionology*. Academic Press.
