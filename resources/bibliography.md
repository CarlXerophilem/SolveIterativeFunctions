# Bibliography — Iterative Functional Equations

## Primary References

1. **Vladimir V. Kruchinin, Dmitry V. Kruchinin** (2013). *Composita and its Properties*. arXiv:1103.2582v2.
   - Introduces the composita \(F^{\Delta}(n,k)\) of ordinary generating functions.

2. **Dmitry V. Kruchinin, Vladimir V. Kruchinin** (2013). *Method for solving an iterative functional equation \(A^{2^n}(x) = F(x)\)*. arXiv:1302.1986v2.
   - Extends the composita method to iterative functional equations and motivates the paper-depth mode in the solver.

## Fractional Iteration and Local Analytic Theory

3. **Gabriel Koenigs** (1884). *Recherches sur les int\u00e9grales de certaines \u00e9quations fonctionnelles*.
   - Classical source for local linearization near a non-parabolic fixed point.

4. **Marek Kuczma, Bogdan Choczewski, Roman Ger** (1990/2008). *Iterative Functional Equations*. Cambridge University Press.
   - Standard reference for the structural theory of iterative functional equations.

5. **V. V. Goryainov** (2002). *Koenigs function and fractional iterates of probability generating functions*.
   - Connects Koenigs coordinates to fractional iteration in a precise analytic setting.

6. **Hellmuth Kneser** (1950). *Reelle analytische L\u00f6sungen der Gleichung \(\varphi(\varphi(x)) = e^x\)*.
   - Classical benchmark for global analytic half-iterate constructions.

## External Resources

7. **Lars Regl\u00f8s**. *f(f(x))* — https://reglos.de/lars/ffx.html
   - Interactive exploration of functional square roots.
